2018 Coffee Quick Facts

  • The total economic impact of the coffee industry in the United States in 2015 was $225.2 billion
  • Coffee-related economic activity comprises approximately 1.6% of the total U.S. gross domestic product
  • Consumers spent $74.2 billion on coffee in 2015
  • The coffee industry is responsible for 1,694,710 jobs in the US economy
  • The coffee industry generates nearly $28 billion in taxes (including ancillary goods)
  • 2nd Most Popular drink after water
  • 1600 million cups a day
  • 250 cups sold per day per store

Case Study: Starbucks Coffee Shops

  • Started in 1971, Pike Place Market. Seatle, WA
  • Since 1987, Starbucks has added an average of 2 stores a day.
  • Santa Fe Springs, CA has the highest concentration of store locations with 560 Starbucks in a 25 mile radius
  • Starbucks uses 2.3 billion paper cups per year.
  • There are more than 300,000+ Starbucks Employees in the world.
